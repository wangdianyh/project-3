Pokémon Types

There are 18 types of 964 Pokémon in Pokémon big data. Click the buttons below graph to view more detail data of each type.

[flying] is the first type of Pokémon in Pokémon world, which has 123 in this type, and it is also the only one type that has over 100 Pokémon.

Obviously, [dragon] is the heighest Pokémon in average.

The weight difference between type is larger than height, and [dragon] wins again in average weight comparison.

Although [dragon] is the biggest Pokémon in average, [psychic] is the heaviest per centimetre. So, a [psychic] Pokémon may look small, but is much heavier than you expect, and never try to hold a [psychic] Pokémon like holding a [Pikachu].

[dangon] gains most base experience in all the types, however, the advantages are not as outstanding as in the comparison of [Average Height] and [Average Weight].
