Damage Class

Pokémon move can be categorized to 3 [damage-class]: physical, status, special. It is noticable that all moves in [status] damage-class have 0 power in attacking, and their accuracy value is much lower than [physical] and [special] too.

Based on the dada result of damage class, the general attacking power of each damage-class should be: [special] > [physical] > [status], and a Pokémon's attacking power in battles mainly depends on moves under [physical] and [special] damage-class.

The Encounter & Catch relationship between Pokémon and Location Area

I selected 96 most easy to catch Pokémon(10%) and 65 most easy to encounter Pokémon location areas(10%) as data sample to compare and analize.

76 out of 96 Pokémon(79.17%) are able to encounter in the top 65 location areas, and 72 out of 96(75%) Pokémon are able to be caught in these 65 location areas.

As a result, the high rate of encountering or catching Pokémon in a certain location area is mainly because of the easy-to-catch Pokémon. In another word, Pokémon apprears in these easy-to-catch location areas is probably those easy-to-catch ones.
